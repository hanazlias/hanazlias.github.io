HOW TO MAKE
CILOR

( Aci dan Telur )

Ingredients:

  • 1.wheat flour
  • 2.tapioca flour
  • 3.chicken stock
  • 4.chili powder
  • 5.salt
  • 6.egg
  • 7.pepper
  • 8.water
  • 9. cooking oil

    • Directions

  • first, put 5 tablespoon of tapioca into the container.
  • second, add 2 tablespoon of wheat flour.
  • third, add chicken stock and salt. and water little by little.
  • after that, stir it evenly
  • then, roll them in and then cut them into pieces
  • cook aci with eggs then scrambled.
  • when it is cooked, sprinkle chili powder.
  • and done! cilor ready to serve
